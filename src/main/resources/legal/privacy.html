Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

IrishPubFinder ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

IrishPubFinder is operated from Northern Ireland, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal data collected through this app. You can contact us at support.irishpubfinder@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: your email address and display name, created when you register.
  • Location data: your device's GPS coordinates, used only while the app is open and only to find nearby pubs. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Visit data: the pubs you mark as visited, including timestamps and any Guinness ratings you submit.
  • Photos: images you upload to pub reviews, stored securely on Cloudflare R2.
  • Usage data: app interactions used to improve the service (e.g. badge events, visualisation preferences).
  • Device data: device type and OS version for technical support and crash diagnostics.
  • Advertising identifiers: in the free version, Google AdMob may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android) to deliver and measure ads. You can reset or limit this in your device settings. Ads are never shown to Pro members.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your data for the following purposes and legal bases:

  • Providing the service (contract): showing nearby pubs, recording visits, awarding badges, and displaying leaderboards.
  • Legitimate interests: preventing abuse, improving app performance, and sending relevant in-app notifications you have opted into.
  • Legal obligation: retaining records as required by UK law.

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties solely to operate the app:

  • Google Places API: your location is sent to Google's servers to retrieve nearby pub listings. Google's Privacy Policy applies to this data (policies.google.com).
  • Cloudflare R2: photos you upload are stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure.
  • App stores (Apple / Google): your purchase of a Pro subscription is processed by Apple or Google. Their privacy policies apply to payment data.
  • Google AdMob: in the free version we show rewarded video ads that you choose to watch to unlock features. AdMob may process your advertising identifier and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. Google's Privacy Policy applies (policies.google.com).

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and visit data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a data subject in the UK, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support.irishpubfinder@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

7. Advertising, Cookies and Analytics

This app does not use cookies and we do not use third-party analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel). The free version uses Google AdMob to show rewarded video ads; AdMob may use device and advertising identifiers to serve and measure those ads. Pro members see no ads.

Where required (for example in the UK and EEA), we ask for your consent before showing personalised ads, and on iOS we request App Tracking Transparency permission. You can change or withdraw your ad-consent choices at any time from Settings → Privacy & Ads → Manage ad consent. Basic crash and performance data may be collected by the Expo framework; this is anonymised and not linked to your account.

8. Children

IrishPubFinder is intended for users aged 18 and over in accordance with legal drinking age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account promptly.

9. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Account passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at support.irishpubfinder@gmail.com.
